Identifying revenue leaks and optimizing your operational efficiency.

What We Audit

Our comprehensive audit is designed to give you a clear picture of your supply chain's health. We look deep into your existing processes to identify areas where you can save costs and improve speed.

1

Sourcing & Quality

We evaluate your material sourcing methods against BOQ requirements and industry quality standards to ensure you're getting the best value.

2

Vendor Ecosystem

Analysis of your current supplier base, their compliance levels, and potential for more reliable, cost-effective alternatives.

3

Logistics Efficiency

A deep dive into your transportation and network setup to find bottlenecks and reduce transit costs.

4

Warehouse Optimization

Review of your storage and distribution processes to improve inventory turnover and reduce holding costs.

5

Digital Maturity

Assessing how much of your procurement is manual vs. digital, and identifying opportunities for automation.

6

Cost (COGS) Analysis

A line-by-line look at your Cost of Goods Sold to find immediate saving opportunities and margin protection strategies.

7

Pre-Bid Strategy

Reviewing your tender support systems to ensure your pre-bid SCM advisory is positioning you to win.

8

Quality Assurance

Evaluating your TPI coordination and QA processes to minimize project delays due to inspection failures.

9

Strategic Resilience

Testing your supply chain's ability to handle disruptions and scale with your business growth.
